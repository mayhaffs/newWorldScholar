Globalization

Globalization is the integration of world culture, communication, financial markets, and ideas. There are many different definitions of the term, "globalization," but everyone has a feeling of what it is. Understanding world politics, the international political economy, significant countries, and global trends is essential to take full advantage of all of the opportunities that are available to us today.
