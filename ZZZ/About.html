About

The purpose of this website is to "encourage self-education with a global perspective and an entrepreneurial mindset." Here at New World Scholar we strive to provide the best educational resources so that ambitious scholars will develop the highest quality technical, conceptual, and interpersonal skills geared for the twenty first century. Our mantra is "Teaching Those, Who Teach Themselves." We do this by encouraging self-education through open-courseware, highlighting trends and analyzing global events, and promoting an optimistic, proactive mindset by showcasing successful entrepreneurs. Do you have the diligence and ambition to become a New World Scholar?

The target audience for this website is not so much based on traditional demographics as it is personality and mindset. The target audience will primarily be students who are highly-motivated, creative, and open-minded. This website is intended for all genders and nationalities. Much of the content is related to the United States, but the goal is to create a global perspective for every student in need.
