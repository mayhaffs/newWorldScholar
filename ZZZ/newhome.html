Welcome to New World Scholar and Michael Mahaffey's Final Website for Introduction to Web Development! Here at New World Scholar we strive to provide the best educational resources so that ambitious scholars will develop the highest quality technical, conceptual, and interpersonal skills geared for the twenty first century. Our mantra is "Teaching Those, Who Teach Themselves." We do this by encouraging self-education through open-courseware, highlighting trends and analyzing global events, and promoting an optimistic, proactive mindset by showcasing successful entrepreneurs.
